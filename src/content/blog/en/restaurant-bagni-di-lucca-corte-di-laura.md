# Bagni di Lucca: History, Nature and Flavors in the Heart of the Serchio Valley

Bagni di Lucca is one of the most fascinating gems of the **Serchio Valley**, a place where history, nature and traditions have been intertwined for centuries. Known since Roman times for its thermal waters, this town experienced its golden age between the 18th and 19th centuries, when it became a favorite destination of European aristocracy, attracted by the mild climate and the healing properties of its spas.

Walking through the hamlets of the municipality, you can still breathe that elegant and timeless atmosphere today. The famous **Casino**, one of the oldest in Europe, the historic villas nestled in greenery, the bridges and villages that dot the territory tell a story of encounters, culture and relaxation.

## What to Visit in Bagni di Lucca

Among the must-see stops are the **Terme di Bagni di Lucca**, a symbol of the territory, ideal for those seeking wellness and tranquility. Nature lovers can explore the numerous trails of the **Val di Lima**, perfect for walks, trekking and moments of pure contact with the most authentic Tuscan landscape.

Don't miss the small surrounding villages, such as **Granaiola (Bagni di Lucca)** and the other hillside hamlets, where time seems to flow more slowly and where you can discover local craftsmanship and a cuisine deeply connected to the land.

## The Gastronomic Tradition of the Serchio Valley

Local cuisine is a journey through the genuine flavors of Tuscany: simple yet full-bodied dishes, local ingredients, fresh pasta, selected meats, mushrooms and truffles that change with the seasons. Eating well here is not just a pleasure, but a true convivial ritual.

And it is precisely in this context, made of history and love for good food, that **La Corte di Laura** was born, the reference point for anyone looking for a **restaurant in Bagni di Lucca** that interprets tradition with passion and attention to quality.
